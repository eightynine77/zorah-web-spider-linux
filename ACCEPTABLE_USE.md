# Acceptable Use Policy (AUP)

## Allowed uses
- Defensive testing of systems you own.
- Security research and education, when you have permission.
- Bug-bounty testing when performed under the rules and scope of the program.

## Forbidden uses
- Scanning, probing, attacking, or otherwise interacting with systems without explicit authorization.
- Bundling or distributing exploit payloads or pentest binaries.
- Turning this software into a hosted scanning or "scanning-as-a-service" offering.
- Any use that violates local laws, the GitHub Acceptable Use Policies, or third-party terms of service.

Maintainers reserve the right to refuse contributions or revoke access for users who violate this policy.
